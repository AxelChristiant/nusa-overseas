Partnership

Supplier

Nusa Overseas opens an opportunity for you to join us to improving your economy and the people around you.


If you are from Indonesia and have agricultural products, know the farmers, or a factory that can process agricultural products, please contact us. We offer a partnership with you that will be an opportunity to sell the products in global market.


Nusa Overseas is very selective when choosing our suppliers so we apply high quality standards with reasonable price to maintain our reputation.


If you want to join us, please contact us by mentioning the type of the products, location, quality detail, production capacity every month (in tons), and the opening price. If we are interested we can work together and make benefit for all parties.
