About Our Company

Our Story

Nusa Overseas was founded by a group of young people who saw the potential of nature around them in early 2021 which was a tough year due to the Covid-19 pandemic. In the beginning, we only wanted to sell products from our factory in Kalimantan Island which is peppercorn, now we have collaborated with more farmers and factories around us to increase the type and quantity of our products.

Nusa Overseas focuses on providing agricultural products, spices, and herbal plants with the best quality and prices. Therefore, our factories are located in several locations depending on the natural commodities in the area.

From many farmer and factory, our products have been trough processes to make it capable to compete in international market. Every process that we were executed on this business based on integrity, honesty, and responsibility to give our customer maximum service because we always prioritizing customer satisfaction of our products.

Vision

We want to become a trustworthiest supplier of Indonesian agriculture products and improve the economy of all participant in this sector.

Goals

  • Create partnerships with farmers and factory to collaborate to produce products that can be compete in the global market
  • Utilizing Indonesia natural comodities.
  • Build up partnership with many parties that participated in this sector from farmer to goverment

Our Company

PT APRA Nusantara Global is a private limited company based in Indonesia. We are focuses on providing agricultural products, spices and herbal plant to fulfill your needs with the best quality and prices. Customer satisfaction is the most important aspects in our business, we always give our customer maximum service based on our integrity, honesty, and responsibility. Therefore, we always ensure that we have to control the production process from the beginning until the product is truly ready to be shipped.


We always aim for sustainable and long-lasting business with all of our partners. We strongly seek to have great cooperation and mutual understanding with our partners, including our customers, farmer, and environment.
